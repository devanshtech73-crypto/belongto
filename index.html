About Us

Earnify was founded with one mission: to make online engagement rewarding for everyone. We understand how valuable your time and attention are, and that’s why we built a system that pays users fairly for interacting with digital content.

Our network connects advertisers with genuine audiences. Each click, view, or task helps advertisers reach real people while users earn from their participation. We are committed to transparent payouts and continuous improvements in user experience.

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. All data collected through our platform, such as visit analytics and redemption records, are securely stored and used only for verification and payment processing purposes.

Your personal information like name, UPI ID, or contact details are encrypted and never shared with third parties. Our servers follow strict industry standards for security and compliance.

Cookies and local storage are used only to save your progress, points, and user preferences. You may clear them anytime without affecting your ability to use our services.

By using Earnify, you agree to our fair-use policy, which ensures balanced earning for all members and transparent communication between advertisers and users.
